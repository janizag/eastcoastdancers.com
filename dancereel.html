My Dance Bio and Video Reel

Janiza Gesmundo started dancing hiphop when she joined her high school’s dance team in the Philippines. She started training at the age of 16 and was trained by mentors from FEU Dance Company, Kinection, The Crew, UP Street Dance Club and more. The Philippine dance community influenced her to keep dancing even after graduating high school.

She moved to New York City in 2012 and started taking hip hop dance classes found from google search. She found RiSE Dance Company who offers hip-hop dance classes in the city, took a beginner’s class and met a few people who introduced her to the New York dance community. She started gaining connections from the community and attended a lot of dance workshops over the summer of 2012.



By the end of 2012, Janiza was able to compete for the first time and perform at various dance events with RiSE Dance Company. She also joined Outburst Dance Company and started a bond together with RiSE and Outburst. She continued the season with being on two teams and later on by the middle of 2013 she decided she wanted to see what the other dance teams offer. She join The Neighbors Dance Team and EPIC Motion Dance Company.

Janiza continued her dance career and was able to perform at lot in 2013. She won World of Dance New Jersey in 2013, traveled and performed at Boston, Virginia, Long Island, New Jersey and back-up danced at big Filipino concert at Prudential Center.



Later in 2014, she’s been a part of Mint Dance Company, Anonymous Dance and UFP Dance Company. Her favorite part being in the dance community aside from placing at dance competitions is meeting new people who share the same passion for dancing. Being in dance community opened a lot of doors for Janiza as she was able to do commercial and professional back-up dancing on the side while also being enrolled in school full time. Janiza danced in music videos, commercials and concerts around the east coast area.
